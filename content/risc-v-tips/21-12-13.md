Physical Memory Attributes (PMAs) define the capabilities of a given address range. A range may be mapped to main memory, I/O devices, or may be empty. PMAs include:

- Access Type
- Atomicity
- Memory-Ordering
- Coherence / Cacheability
- Idempotency
