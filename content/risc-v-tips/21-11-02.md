Tonight's @risc_v Tip:

You can use two convenient pseudo-instructions to load the address of a symbol into a CSR.

`la t1, main` => `auipc t1, main[31:12]; addi t1, t1, main[11:0]`

`csrw mepc, t1` => `csrrw x0, mepc, t1`
